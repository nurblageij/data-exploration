Here you can find a collection *Python* notebooks providing examples of data visualization and exploration.


## Data visualization

To start with, we consider three popular datasets on the *UCI* repository.

#### [*Iris* dataset](./visualization_iris.ipynb)
The [*Iris* dataset](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/53/iris) contains physical measurements of iris flowers, as the predictive attributes, accompanied by the indication of the species each specimen belongs to, as the class. It is the main example dataset used in part I of the course, on classification.

#### [*Dry Bean* dataset](./visualization_drybean.ipynb)
The [*Dry Bean* dataset](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/602/dry+bean+dataset) contains measurements automatically derived from digital images of bry beans taken following a standardized protocol using a high-resolution camera. It is the main example dataset used in part II of the course, on clustering.

#### [*Glass Identification* dataset](./visualization_glass.ipynb)
The [*Glass Identification* dataset](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/42/glass+identification) contains information about the chemical properties of glass samples and the task typically considered is to predict the type of glass of each sample.

We continue with three datasets belonging to the area of health and medicine.

#### [*Heart Failure* dataset](./visualization_heart-failure.ipynb)
The [*Heart Failure* dataset](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/519/heart+failure+clinical+records) contains medical records of heart failure patients.

#### [*Maternal Health Risk* dataset](./visualization_maternal-health-risk.ipynb)
The [*Maternal Health Risk* dataset](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/863/maternal+health+risk) contains monitoring data of pregnant women collected mostly in a rural area of Bangladesh, with the purpose of estimating the risk of maternal mortality.

#### [*National Poll on Healthy Aging (NPHA)* dataset](./visualization_NPHA.ipynb)
The [*National Poll on Healthy Aging (NPHA)* dataset](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/936/national+poll+on+healthy+aging+(npha)) contains features related to health and sleep in older American adults.


We then turn to two datasets belonging to the area of learning analytics.

#### [*Students' Dropout*](./visualization_students-dropout.ipynb)
The [*Students' Dropout*](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/697/predict+students+dropout+and+academic+success) dataset contains information about the demographics and socioeconomic background, as well as the academic path and performance of students enrolled in different undergraduate degrees at an institution of higher education in Portugal.

#### [*Student Performance* dataset](./visualization_student-performance.ipynb)
The [*Student Performance* dataset](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/320/student+performance) contains demographic and social background as well as grades of students from two secondary schools in Portugal. It consists of two tables corresponding to two different topics, namely mathematics and Portuguese language. Before turning to visualizing the data, we show how to merge the two tables.

The visualizations for the seven datasets above, in this order, are also available as [a single notebook](./visualization_datasets.ipynb).

## Data exploration

The last two notebooks also start from datasets available on the *UCI* repository, but provide a more in-depth exploration, drawing on additional sources to enrich the data and using geospatial visualizations, i.e. maps.

#### [*Plants* dataset](./explore_plants.ipynb)
The [*Plants* dataset](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/180/plants) has been prepared from data extracted from the *USDA Plant List of Attributes, Names, Taxonomy, and Symbols (PLANTS)* database. It records the distribution of plant taxa across North America. We combine it with the geographic coordinates of the locations to draw maps showing the distribution of plants.

#### [*Congressional Voting Records* dataset](./explore_house-votes-84.ipynb)
The [*Congressional Voting Records* dataset](https://archive.ics.uci.edu/dataset/105/congressional+voting+records) records the votes cast by elected member of the United States House of Representatives for sixteen key votes of the taken during the second session of the 98th United States Congress, in 1984.
